Games

The following are a list of games that have been made. For most of them, I've done most of the programming, design, and art, etc. However, in many of them, I've also made use of free and available art resources, especially games made for jams, due to the tight constraints on time.

I enjoy making games that can be easily deployed and disseminated - so that means being able to play them right in the browser with minimal fuss (including plugins like Flash and Unity Web Player.) Thus, most of them are made in HTML5.
